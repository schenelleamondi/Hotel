Our History

Hoteles Huida was built and brought up by one of the greatest people in Latin America known as Horge Salvador in the early '80s.

The Hotel, for Mr. Salvador, is a place where people can get away from their troubles and have a moment of peace, hence the name Hoteles Huida, meaning Hotel of Escape.
